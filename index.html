❓ Tips & FAQ
📍 GPS location doesn't work — why?
When you tap Allow location, your browser asks permission. A few things can block it:

• You tapped Don't Allow before → go to your phone's Settings → Safari (or Chrome) → Location → Allow
• You're in Private / Incognito mode → GPS is often blocked there. Open the app in a normal tab.
• iPhone + Chrome → iOS restricts GPS in Chrome. Try Safari instead for best results.
• Location Services off on your phone → Settings → Privacy → Location Services → On
🏠 How do I set my home location without GPS?
Go to the 🌍 World tab → pick your city from the dropdown → tap 🏠 Set as My Home.

Once set, the app uses that city for your daylight bar and season — even without GPS. Tap 🔓 to lock it so you don't change it by accident.
🌍 Why does my season look wrong?
By default the app uses northern hemisphere seasons. If you live in the southern hemisphere (Australia, New Zealand, South America, South Africa) set your home city in the 🌍 World tab — the season will automatically flip.

Tropical locations (near the equator) show Rainy / Dry season instead.
🌅 How does sunrise/sunset work?
The app calculates real sunrise and sunset times using your location's latitude and longitude — no internet needed. This means in winter the scene gets dark earlier, and in summer it stays light longer, just like outside. Near the poles you may see polar day (sun never sets) or polar night (sun never rises).
🐾 How do I change my friend's avatar or name?
In the 🌍 World tab under Friend clocks:

• Tap the avatar icon 🐶 to cycle through dog emojis
• Tap the friend's name ✏️ to rename them
• Use ▲ ▼ arrows to reorder the list
• Tap ✕ to remove a friend
📱 Changing phone — how to keep my data?
Your data lives on your device, so when changing phones follow these steps:

On your old phone:
1. Go to 📊 Stats tab → tap 📤 Backup
2. A text block appears — copy it all
3. Paste it somewhere safe: Notes app, email to yourself, or a message

On your new phone:
1. Open the app in your browser
2. Go to 📊 Stats → tap 📥 Restore
3. Paste the backup text → tap OK
4. All your steps, walks, streak, puppy name, home city and friends come back ✓

💡 Good habit: back up after a long walk streak so you never lose it!
🔒 Is my data private?
Yes — everything stays on your device. No account, no server, no tracking. Location is only used locally to calculate sunrise/sunset and fetch weather (via a privacy-friendly open service). Nothing is sent anywhere without your action.
