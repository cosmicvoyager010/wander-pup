📱 Apple Shortcuts setup
One-time setup (2 min):
1. Open Shortcuts app → New Shortcut
2. Add: Get Health Samples (type: Steps, period: Today)
3. Add: Open URL → paste your app URL
4. Add shortcut to Home Screen

Daily use: Tap shortcut when on WiFi → steps auto-fill below 🐾
